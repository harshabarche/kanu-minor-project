Introduction of Exam Genius

Intelligent Exam Seating Management(IESM) This project provides a way to allocate exam hall for
each students of various branches and sections without any clash. It also keep a check at the duties of various teachers at different classes during exams.
In these project has been developed to over ride the problem prevailing in practicing manual system.
This website is supported to eliminate and reduce faced by existing system.
The system stores list of classes, teachers and capacity of rooms and list of students appearing
the exam on each day.
The system also provide a facility of getting a chart of allotment to administrator and a email
notification of allotment of every individual.
